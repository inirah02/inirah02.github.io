## Abstract

This paper presents an empirical study examining the effectiveness of Financial BERT models for sentiment analysis in cryptocurrency markets and their correlation with price movements.

- Comparative analysis of BERT-based models for financial sentiment analysis
- Investigation of sentiment-price correlations in cryptocurrency markets
